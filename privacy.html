Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

At NetParGolf, we believe your personal game data belongs to you. This privacy policy describes our approach to security, data tracking, and platform collection guidelines.

1. Complete Local Storage Architecture

NetParGolf is designed intentionally as a fully offline utility app. All course information, player details, round scores and round history are stored locally on your device.

We do not maintain cloud sync systems, remote platform servers, or central user accounts. Your information never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Networks & Privacy Scans

To preserve your processing performance and system anonymity, NetParGolf enforces the following guardrails:

  • No Advertising Networks: We do not integrate programmatic ad trackers, marketing profiles, or identifier kits.
  • No Analytics Engines: We run no background behavior counters, screen tracking scripts, or profiling analytics networks.

3. Data Retainment & Erasure

Because your files exist completely under your native ecosystem's storage framework, removing the NetParGolf application completely from your smartphone immediately deletes all local history caches and course layouts.

4. Contact Enquiries

For questions or verification reviews concerning the app structure, please contact our publisher team directly via the contact methods listed on our dedicated Support Page.
