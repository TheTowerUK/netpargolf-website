Frequently Asked Questions

How do I fix a "Stroke Index missing" alert?

Navigate to Course Setup from the dashboard panel, select your target course, and click Edit. Key in the missing values for each individual hole layout and hit Save. The alert will disappear and point calculations will update correctly.

Where is my data backed up?

To maximize privacy, all profiles, historical rounds, and layouts are stored 100% locally on your specific phone. They are not backed up to an external cloud database network.

Are my competition formats supported?

The app features automated math rules for 4 modes: Individual Stableford, Betterball Stableford, Singles Matchplay, and Fourball Betterball Matchplay.
